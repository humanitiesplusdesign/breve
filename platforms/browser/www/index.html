Breve is a tool for research. Use it to quickly review and edit data tables.
To learn about all of the features, visit http://breve.designhumanities.org.

This is version 0.0.2. Please tweet us or email us with your ideas about how to make this tool better.

Breve was created at the Humanities + Design research lab at Stanford University. Check out our other data visualization tool, Palladio.
